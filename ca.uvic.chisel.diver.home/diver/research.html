Diver is Research

One of the reasons that Diver is free for your use is that it is a research project of the Computer Human Interaction Software Engineering Lab (CHISEL) at the University of Victoria. It is used for partial fulfilment of the M.Sc. work for Master's student Del Myers.

But don't let the fact that this is a research project scare you. Years of research and development have already gone into this project, and we try to make it of the highest quality. CHISEL is dedicated to researching how to build software that works well for people, and we apply the lessons that we learn. But, if you find any problems, or if you would like to see more features, don't hesitate to file an issue using Trac
